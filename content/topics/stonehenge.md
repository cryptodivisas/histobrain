## Overview
Stonehenge is one of the most mysterious prehistoric monuments in the world. Located in England, it consists of massive standing stones arranged in a circular formation. Its purpose continues to be debated.

## Key Facts
- Location: Wiltshire, England  
- Built: c. 3000–2000 BC  
- Material: Stone  
- Type: Monument  
- UNESCO Site: Yes  

## History
Stonehenge was built in several stages over centuries. It is believed to have been used for rituals or ceremonies.

## Structure
The stones are arranged in circular patterns with precise alignment to the sun. Some stones were transported from distant locations.

## Impact / Legacy
Stonehenge remains a symbol of ancient ingenuity and mystery. It attracts visitors and researchers worldwide.

## Did You Know?
- Some stones came from over 200 miles away.  
- It aligns with the summer solstice sunrise.  
- Its purpose is still debated.  
- It is older than the pyramids.
