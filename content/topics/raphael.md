## Overview

Raphael was one of the leading artists of the High Renaissance. His work is celebrated for balance, harmony, and clarity, representing the ideals of classical beauty.

## Early Life

Born in Urbino, Italy, Raphael trained under respected artists and quickly developed his own style. His early work showed remarkable skill and refinement.

## Major Works

His most famous work, *The School of Athens*, depicts great philosophers in a grand architectural setting. It is considered a masterpiece of Renaissance art.

## Style

Raphael’s paintings emphasize proportion, calm expression, and balanced composition, setting him apart from other artists.

## Legacy

Despite his short life, Raphael’s influence on art was profound and long-lasting.

## Did You Know?

- He died at age 37.  
- He worked extensively in the Vatican.  
- He is one of the three Renaissance masters.  
- His style is known for harmony and clarity.
